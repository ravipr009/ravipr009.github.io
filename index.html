Ravi Prakash

Hello there!, I am a Postdoc working in Interactive Imitation Learning at the LAIR: Learning, Autonomous, and Intelligent Robots group, Cognitive Robotics, TU Delft headed by Prof. Jens Kober . My research interests revolve around Imitation Learning and Neural Control for robot manipulator. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Laxmidhar Behera during my PhD from Control & Automation group, Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur, India.

Prior to my graduate studies, I received Institute Silver Medal for the top performing bachelor's student in Electrical Engineering from NIT Silchar .

Selected Projects

Teaching Robots Interactively (TERI)
Programming and re-programming robots is extremely time-consuming and expensive, which presents a major bottleneck for new industrial, agricultural, care, and household robot applications. My goal is to realize a scientific breakthrough in enabling robots to learn how to perform manipulation tasks from few human demonstrations, based on novel interactive machine learning techniques.
